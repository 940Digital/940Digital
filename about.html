Built to grow

Owen Leiter, Founder

940Digital is built on a straightforward idea: contractors deserve a digital partner who's invested in their growth — not just hired to check a box. Most web agencies hand over a site and disappear. We stay, because your results are our results.

We work with electricians, tree services, HVAC techs, roofers, and the trades that keep neighborhoods running. These businesses don't need a marketing department — they need a website that brings in calls, SEO that puts them where customers are searching, and support that doesn't disappear once the site is live.

I've wanted to build things people actually want to use for as long as I can remember. My first job was at a marketing agency, where I picked up SEO, design, and development from the ground up — and somewhere in there, the skill set turned into the actual reason I do this: watching a business I worked with take a real next step, and knowing I had something to do with it.

That's still the whole job. I'm not interested in being another vendor on your invoice. I want to be the reason your business had room to grow.

Invested, not just hired

Built for trades, not tech
